About Us

Hi world, we are a region located in Indonesia. As we know, Indonesia has a wealth of flora & fauna. Geographically our area is surrounded by teak forests that have grown for tens to hundreds of years. That is why the majority of our professions are craftsmen specialized in teak wood roots for decades from generation to generation. Spread over 30 locations and more than 100 experienced craftsmen.

Thriving in a tropical climate, especially in our area, has been known to have good quality. It has a more textured & lively 3D color, low moisture content & sapwood, plus strength, durability, and termite resistance. Combined with the experience of our craftsmen, we create masterpieces that excel in both quality and durability.

Seeing our potential, we are going global. Now we are not only available in the local market but also internationally. From wasted roots to Masterpieces!

Our Values

Sustainability & Eco-Friendly

We use reclaimed teak wood from legal, well-managed forests under Perhutani supervision, ensuring a fully traceable and sustainable supply chain. Nothing is wasted — every root becomes art

“Responsibly made. Naturally beautiful”

Direct From Artisan

We cut out the middleman to give you better prices and a closer connection to the maker. Authenticity meets efficiency

“From village to villa — directly.”

Empowering Local Hand

Each piece is handcrafted by artisans from Java’s rural highlands. By working directly with our communities, we preserve tradition while creating real economic impact

“Crafted by hands. Sustained by heart.”

Fast & Reliable Lead Time

With over 25 years of experience, and a network of 93 skilled artisans across 33 production sites, we ensure timely delivery without compromising on quality

“Decades of mastery. Days to deliver.“

Wooden Animal Sculpture: Sustainable Art from Reclaimed Teak Root

Wooden animal sculptures are not just decorative items—they are a fusion of nature, sustainability, and craftsmanship. At Rootecraft, every sculpture is handcrafted from 100% reclaimed teak root, giving new life to discarded wood while preserving Indonesia’s rich artisanal tradition. Today, these sculptures are widely exported to Europe, the UK, and Australia, where eco-friendly and unique home décor is highly valued.
