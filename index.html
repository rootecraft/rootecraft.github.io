About Us

Hi world, we are a region located in Indonesia. As we know Indonesia has a wealth of flora & fauna. Geographically our area is surrounded by teak forests that have grown for tens to hundreds of years. That is why the majority of our professions is craftsmen speciallized in teak wood roots for decades from generation to generation. Spread over 30 locations and more than 100 experienced craftsmen Thriving in a tropical climate, especially in our area, has been known to have good quality. That has a more textured & lively 3d color, phas a low moisture content & sapwood, also has strength, durability and termite resistant.

The good quality & quantity combined with the experienced craftsmen's hand create a masterpiece that is not only good in terms of quality but also in terms of strength & durability. Seeing our potential, we are going to the world. Now we are not only available in the local market, but also internationally. This is our best. From wasted roots to Masterpieces!
